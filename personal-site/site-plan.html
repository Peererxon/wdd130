FAQ

How much does a website cost?

This is the most popular question I'm asked. But there’s a problem when it comes to answering. The truth is I don’t know how much your website will cost until I know what sort of website you need! Each website and client is different, and my costs are based on your exact requirements. When I know what you need, I’ll provide a transparent, no-obligation quote so you know exactly what you’re paying for

How long will my site take to build?

Another question I get asked a lot. Again, this varies depending on your exact requirements. Some projects can take a few months while others a few weeks. Once I’ve discussed your project, I’ll give you an idea of just how long the project will take.

What is web hosting?

Web hosting is the service that keeps your website online, and it’s an important consideration for any successful online business. It can be daunting to choose the right provider as there are just so many web hosting companies to pick from. Your website needs a reliable, affordable and dependable host – preferably one that regularly backs up your website and keeps your files safe and secure.

Are there any other hidden costs for my website?

No. That’s a promise. I don’t believe in hidden fees or unexpected bills.
